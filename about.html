About Us

Welcome to the home page of the Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance Inc. USA Dance, Inc. is a non-profit national organization that encourages self-expression through ballroom dancing, socially as well as competitively. We believe that our focus on ballroom dancing contributes to the mental and physical aspects of well-being. In short-it's good for your body, heart, mind and spirit!

The Southwest Florida Chapter (#6049) was established in 1995 and has been twice honored as Chapter of the Year.* Our chapter operates on a year-around basis serving Lee and Collier counties, including Fort Myers, Estero and Naples.

In 2020 we celebrate or 25th anniversary! During this time, we have grown to become the second largest chapter in Florida, which we attribute to our focus on dance education and social dance events. We encourage dancers to participate in the physical, mental and social benefits of ballroom dancing through:

  • Affordable Group Classes for Newcomers and Beginners (and dancers that want to refine their foundational technique). (link to Dance Education page)
  • Beyond Basics: Classes for dancers that are ready to take the next step in their dance education.
  • Social Dances: Monthly social events where everyone at any skill level can meet and enjoy dancing together. We encourage all to participate.
  • Formal Dances: Twice yearly special events where “black-tie and cocktail/gowns” are the attire for the evening. Dinner and live music create an elegant evening.
  • Workshops: Periodically held throughout the year, workshops are single day/afternoon events designed to enhance technique and add a few new steps.
